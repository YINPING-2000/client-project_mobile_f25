What is career exploration?

Career exploration is the process of researching and evaluating different career options to find the best fit for your skills, interests, and values. It involves gathering information about various industries, job roles, and career paths to make informed decisions about your future.

Using AI to Explore Careers

Not sure what career path suits you best? AI can suggest potential careers, provide insights on job market trends, and help you identify relevant skills for different industries. The key to using AI effectively is to explore multiple options, ask targeted questions about specific fields, and use AI-generated insights as a starting point for deeper research.

Try to use these prompts:

  • Prompt 1: “My values are [insert values] …”
    • “What job titles fall under these career paths?”
    • “Generally, what types of classes could I look at taking …?”
    • “What other jobs are similar to [insert job title] …?”
  • Prompt 2: “I want to live in [insert location] …”
    • “What is [organization or industry name’s] typical entry-level salary …?”
    • “What is the average cost of living in [insert location]?”
    • “Provide me with search resources to gather information …”
  • Prompt 3: “I have skills in [insert skills] …”
    • “What are the common skills required for these job titles?”
    • “What are the typical career paths for someone starting in …?”
    • “What are some potential challenges I might face …?”

Career Exploration FAQ

  • How do I start exploring careers?
    Begin by assessing your interests, skills, and values. Use online resources, attend career fairs, and connect with professionals in fields that interest you.
  • What resources are available for career exploration?
    UMSI offers various resources including career counseling, workshops, alumni panels, and online tools to help you explore different career paths.
  • How can I make informed decisions about my career path?
    Research industries and job roles, seek advice from mentors and professionals, and consider internships or volunteer opportunities to gain practical experience.
